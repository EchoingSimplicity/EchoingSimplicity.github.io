Who Is Asher Parker?

I'm a third year college student studying Software Programming at Bellevue College.

What do you like to do?
I enjoy reading, writing, hiking, running, and cooking.

Why programming?
My family is very tech-oriented with a number of them working programming jobs, so it was a natural progression for me. When I tried out programming, I found myself getting sucked into it for hours on end where others might get frustrated. That was a pretty good clue that programming is the right fit for me.

What do you hope to do with your programming degree?
Hopefully I'll be able to work for a company that has a good purpose, such as charity or providing essential services.
